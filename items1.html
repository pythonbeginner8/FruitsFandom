1. Sword

In the vast, treacherous seas of the Blox Fruits world, there existed a legend whispered among pirates and marines alike: a tale of swords so powerful they could cleave the heavens and split the ocean. These weapons, wielded by only the most skilled swordsmen, were said to hold the essence of mythical beasts and ancient warriors, their power rivaling even the most potent Blox Fruits.
