Fruits
Types
Ratings/Details

Bomb Fruit
Common
 +) Cheapest fruit ever, also the most suitable fruit for beginners.
+) Bomb has relatively good damage for a Common fruit.
+) Does not have any long range moves.
+) User is immobile while holding their moves, leaving room for attack.

Ice Fruit
Uncommon
 +) Elemental fruit (good for farming due to its immunity and stuns).
+) All moves have good AoE and the [V] move covers a wide radius.
+) Passive allows the user to walk on Water.
+) Extremely weak in air.

Light Fruit
Rare
 +) Elemental fruit (good for farming due to its immunity and stuns).
+) Long-ranged attacks, which give it a versatile moveset.
+) Fastest, strongest fruit in game.
+) All moves do not break Instinct (Obversation Haki).

Dragon Fruit
Mythical
 +) Good combo potential.
+) Big AOE, good damage.
+) Most expensive fruit to purchase, hardest to master in game.
+) Overall, a very good grinding fruit (especially good in hybrid form).
