 I. Introduction

- The Light Fruit is a popular Logia-type fruit in Blox Fruits, offering exceptional speed, powerful ranged attacks, and unique utility skills. This fruit costs $650,000 or 1100 Robux from the Blox Fruit Dealer.

- It is inspired by the Pika Pika no Mi fruit in One Piece manga. In One Piece, This Logia-type Devil Fruit is used by Admiral Kizaru, granting him the power of light manipulation.

- Players who consume the Ice Fruit would obtain superhuman speed likened to light, emit beams of light and travel across vast distances in a flash.

II. Moves

- This fruit is really special since it has two versions. You can unlock the awakened version of light fruit by defeating RAID .

- [Z] Light Beam: Fires a concentrated beam of light, dealing high damage to a single target.

- [X] Light Barrage: Unleashes a rapid series of light projectiles, covering a wide area.

- [C] Mirror Kick: A teleporting kick that delivers devastating damage to enemies in its path.

- [V] Sky Beam Barrage: Rains down multiple beams of light from above, targeting a wide area.

- [F] Light Flight: Allows high-speed travel by turning the user into light.



III. Pros and cons

Strengths
Weaknesses
+) The unawakened version has straightforward moves, kinda fast (use Portal instead if you have not awakened yet).
+) Grants invincibility against non-Haki attacks, perfect for grinding early-game. (it is MONEY babeee)
+) Smaller attack areas, not suitable anymore if you are at a high level (1200 for examples).
+) All skills are easy to dodge, it is extremely ineffective while dealing with pro players having Observation haki (both v1 and v2).
+) Unawakened skills deal less damage, it is almost as useless as common fruits in game if you have never unlocked the awakened version (especially in Second or Third Sea).
