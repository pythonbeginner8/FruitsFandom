Fighting Styles


In the vast seas of the Blox Fruits world, adventurers, pirates, and marines constantly sought the ultimate power to dominate their rivals. Among these powers, none were as revered and diverse as the Fighting Styles, each with its own story, legacy, and mastery techniques.
Fighting styles

Ratings/Details

Combat
Common
 +) Easy to use, low end-lag, slight knockback.
+) Terrible damage.
+) Only two abilities, unlike other fighting styles with three or four abilities

Super Human
Epic
 +) Extremly good combo potential, typical fighting style.
+) All moves can be used in a combo for excellent damage output.
+) Great for Raids since the player can use the Z move to stun the Boss so others can hit them.
+) Players need to grind 4 fighting styles to 300 mastery) and need to be in Second Sea or above.

Electric Claw
Legendary
 +) Good combo potential. All moves low end-lag, making it hard to punish. All moves are able to multi-hit targets.
+) Good for grinding.
+) High mastery requirements.
+) Difficult to obtain, hard to perform combos

Godhuman
Mythical
 +) High combo potential, very high damage, instant reaction
+) Skill X has big AOE, good damage.
+) Extremely hard to obtain (req. 400 mastery on every fighting styles, high costs)
+) The moves are extremely bad for grinding normal enemies, but good for raids and boss fights.
