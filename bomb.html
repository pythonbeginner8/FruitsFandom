 I. Introduction

- The Bomb Fruit is a Common Natural-type Blox Fruit in Blox Fruits, available for $80,000 or 220 Robux from the Blox Fruit Dealer (the cheapest fruit in the game).

- It is inspired by the Bomu-Bomu No Mi fruit in One Piece manga. In One Piece, the Bomu Bomu no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit eaten by Mr. 5 (Gem). It grants the user the ability to make their body explode at will without harming themselves, effectively turning them into a human bomb.

II. Moves

- Targeted Bomb (Z): A ranged attack that explodes at a specific spot. You can hold it for extra damage, making it useful for targeting enemies from a distance.

- Bomb Grab (X): This lets you dash forward and grab an enemy, causing an explosion and flinging them around. It’s great for both offense and mobility during close combat.

- Land Mines (C): You can set up invisible explosive traps that detonate when enemies step on them. It's a strategic move for ambushes or area control.

- Self Destruct (V): This is the ultimate move where you charge up an explosion around yourself. The longer you hold it, the more damage it deals, but it drains your health while charging. It’s risky but devastating when timed correctly.
