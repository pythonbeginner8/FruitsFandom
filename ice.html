 I. Introduction

- The Ice Fruit in Blox Fruits is a Rare Elemental (Logia) fruit available for $350,000 or 750 Robux from the Blox Fruit Dealer.

- It is inspired by the Hie-Hie No Mi fruit in One Piece manga. In One Piece, the fruit mirrors the abilities of Admiral Aokiji, known for his chilling powers and ice-based combat techniques.

- Players who consume the Ice Fruit gain immunity to basic attacks from lower-level enemies and unlock powerful ice-themed skills that are perfect for grinding, PvP battles, and exploration.

II. Moves

- This fruit is really special since it has two versions. You can unlock the awakened version of ice fruit by defeating RAID .

- Ice Spears (Z): Launches sharp ice projectiles at enemies.

- Glacial Surge (X): Creates an ice eruption that damages and freezes opponents.

- Ice Floor (C): Freezes the ground, immobilizing enemies within range.

- Ice Dragon (V): Summons a massive ice dragon that charges forward, dealing significant damage.
